Who We Are

Our Mission & Vision

Hearts United Nepal Org was founded with the belief that every Nepali deserves access to basic needs — healthcare, education, and dignity. We work tirelessly to bridge the gap between those in need and those who can help.

Our vision is a Nepal where every child can go to school, every family has access to healthcare, and no one goes to sleep hungry. Since our founding in 2016, we have been steadily working towards that vision.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These values guide every decision we make and every action we take in service of our communities.

Compassion

We approach every individual and community with empathy, respect, and genuine care for their wellbeing.

Integrity

Full transparency in how we use donations and resources, with regular public reporting of our work.

Community First

We work with communities, not just for them. Local voices lead our programs and initiatives.

Innovation

Continuously improving our methods and embracing new solutions to address social challenges.

Sustainability

Building long-term impact through programs that create lasting, self-sustaining change.

Partnership

Collaborating with local and international organizations to amplify our impact across Nepal.

Our Journey

Our Milestones

From humble beginnings to a growing movement — our journey of impact over the years.

2016

Organization Founded

Hearts United Nepal Org was established with a small team of passionate volunteers in Kathmandu.

2017

First Education Drive

Distributed 500 school kits to underprivileged students in Sindhupalchok district.

2019

First Medical Camp

Organized free medical camp serving 800+ patients in remote communities of Dolakha.

2021

100+ Volunteers

Reached a milestone of 100 registered volunteers across 8 districts of Nepal.

2024

5,000 Lives Impacted

Celebrated reaching 5,000+ lives impacted across 12 districts with 45+ completed projects.
