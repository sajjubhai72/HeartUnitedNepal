Become a Volunteer

Your time, skills, and passion can transform lives across Nepal. Join our growing volunteer family.

Why Volunteer?

What You'll Gain

Volunteering with us is a rewarding experience that goes far beyond giving back.

Recognition

Official certificate of participation and a reference letter from our organization.

Training

Access to skill-building workshops, field training, and professional development programs.

Network

Connect with like-minded individuals, NGO professionals, and changemakers across Nepal.

Purpose

Experience the deep fulfillment of directly changing lives in your own community.

Opportunities

Volunteer Areas

Education

Tutoring, mentorship, and literacy programs for children in rural areas.

Healthcare

Support medical camps as a helper, data entry volunteer, or patient coordinator.

Media & Documentation

Photography, videography, and storytelling to spread our mission.

Logistics & Field Work

Help organize distributions, manage events, and support field operations.
