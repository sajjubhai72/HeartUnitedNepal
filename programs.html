All Programs

Each program is designed with community needs in mind — sustainable, impactful, and community-led.

Education Support Program

We provide scholarships, school supplies, and mentorship to underprivileged students across rural Nepal. Our goal is to ensure no child misses out on education due to financial hardship.

  • Scholarships for 200+ students annually
  • School kit distribution drives
  • After-school tutoring & mentorship
Support This Program

Medical Camp Program

We organize free health checkups, provide medicines, and run awareness programs in remote areas of Nepal that have limited or no access to healthcare facilities.

  • 10+ medical camps per year
  • Free medicines & consultations
  • Health awareness for women & children
Support This Program

Food & Nutrition Program

Providing nutritious meals and food packages to families facing poverty and food insecurity, particularly in earthquake-affected and flood-prone areas of Nepal.

  • Monthly food package distributions
  • School meal programs
  • Nutrition awareness workshops
